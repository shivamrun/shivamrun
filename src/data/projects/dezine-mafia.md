Dezine Mafia is built for designers who want a cleaner place to discover work, share inspiration, and connect with opportunities. It brings together thoughtful portfolios, useful tools, and curated references in one public-facing ecosystem.

## What it does

- Helps designers showcase portfolios in a dedicated directory.
- Organizes creative tools and inspiration in one place.
- Creates a public home for designers who build and share their work openly.

## Product direction

- Encourages discovery across three focused lanes: inspirations, tools, and portfolios.
- Highlights quality through moderated public submissions before content goes live.
- Makes browsing and submitting feel simpler for both creators and visitors.

## Why it matters

- Reduces the friction of finding strong creative references.
- Gives designers a more intentional place to present their work.
- Connects discovery, inspiration, and professional visibility in one product.
